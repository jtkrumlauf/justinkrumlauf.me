What is CS:GO?

  Counter Strike: Global Offensive is a first person shooter released in August of 2012 by Valve. It is a team-based shooter where players must either eliminate their opponents or, in the case of the Terrorist side, plant and detonate a bomb. Counter Strike was originally released as a mod for Half-Life in 1999. Through each of its iterations- 1.6, Source and now Global Offensive- Counter-Strike has become the benchmark for player skill and the basis for all first person shooters.

  Teams from around the world are constantly showcasing their abilities and stratigies at local, regional, and international tournements. Professional Counter-Strike players have become legendary in the international community, the video on the right, showcases a handful of moments from past events.

Although simple on the surface, Counter Strike takes thousands of hours to master, even then, there is always something new to learn.

What are Skins?

  One of the most major additions for CS:GO, skins were released with the "Arms-Deal" update on August 13th 2013. The update added cosmetic weapon finishes that are obtainable by unboxing cases using in-game keys or through random drops. These skins helped form a virtual economy for CS:GO. These skins can range from anywhere between $.03 to upwards of $5,000

Today, skins are regularly traded between players for both real life money and other skins of equal value, creating a vast virtual economy. Some sources say that the total value of the CS:GO skin economy is over 23 million dollars.
